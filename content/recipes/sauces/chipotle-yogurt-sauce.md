### Ingredients

<div class="ingredient-list">

3 cloves garlic  
2-3 tbsp chipotle en adobo  
1 tsp ground cumin  
1 tsp ground coriander  
1 tsp smoked paprika   
1/2 tsp salt  
1 cup Greek yogurt  
1/4 cup mayonnaise  

</div>

### Instructions
Put everything in a blender and blend until emulsified. Correct seasoning as needed.
