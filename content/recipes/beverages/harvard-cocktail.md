### Ingredients

<div class="ingredient-list">

* 3 oz cognac
* 2 oz sweet vermouth
* 3-4 dashes Angostura bitters
* Club soda

</div>

### Instructions
Stir well over cracked ice, then strain into a chilled cocktail glass. Top with an ounce or so of club soda or seltzer.
