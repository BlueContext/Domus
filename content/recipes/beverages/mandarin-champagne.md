### Ingredients

<div class="ingredient-list">

* 2 tsp Mandarin Napoleon liqueur
* Chilled Champagne 
* 1 segment mandarin orange for garnish

</div>

### Instructions
In a stemmed glass, pour the liqueur and top the glass off with Champagne. Garnish with the mandarin segment.
